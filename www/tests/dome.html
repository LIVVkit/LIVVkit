dome

3-D paraboloid dome of ice with a circular, 60 km diameter base sitting on a flat bed. The horizontal spatial resolution studies are 2 km, 1 km, 0.5 km and 0.25 km, and there are 10 vertical levels. For this set of experiments a quasi no-slip basal condition in imposed by setting. A zero-flux boundary condition is applied to the dome margins.
